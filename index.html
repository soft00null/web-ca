Who we are

CA. Swarup Chingale, the founder of Chingale & Associates, is a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India and set up practice at Pune, MH IN. Chingale & Associates recognised for his role as one of the pioneers of the Chartered Accountancy practice in India. Ever since our inception, our mission has been to render professional services of the highest standard to clients, upholding the code of conduct and ethics of the profession.
